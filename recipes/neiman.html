Neiman Marcus Cake

Looking for a cake recipe that will give you something like the ooey, gooey butter cake you had a Neiman Marcus? Try this one!

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 4 large eggs, divided
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspon vanilla extract
  • 3 Â½ cups confectioners' sugar
  • 8 ounces chesee cream, softened

Directions

Step 1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 13x9-inch baking pan.

Step 2 Combine yellow cake mix, 2 eggs, butter, and vanilla in a large bowl; mix well. The batter will be stiff. Add cake batter to the prepared pan and spread evenly.

Step 3 Combine confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, and the remaining 2 eggs in a separate bowl; mix well and pour over the top of the cake batter.

Step 4 Bake the cake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 35 minutes.

Step 5 Let cake cool completely before you cut it to achieve those delicious layers.
